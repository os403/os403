## Morning Routine (1h 30m)

* Wake Up
* Breathing [20 mins]
* Get up from bed
* Throw trash & Pack
* Walk [30 mins - 2KM]
* Cleanup Desk
* Fill Water bottle
* Charge:
    * Watch, phone, headset, tablet
* Brush [5 mins]
* Bath [20 min]
* Wear watch
