## Morning Routine (1h 30m)

* Wake Up
* Breathing (20 mins)
* Get up from bed
* Throw trash & Pack
* Walk (30 mins - 2KM)
* Cleanup Desk
* Fill Water bottle
* Charge:
    * Watch, phone, headset, tablet
* Brush (5 mins)
* Bath (20 min)
* Wear watch

## Night Routine 

* Face wash
* Read tomorrow's agenda (From work profile)
* Close all chrome tabs
* Log out, Turn off
* Breathing (20 mins)
* Turn off lights and fans
